Here are a few instructions to help navigate your way around FruitFolio.
FULL SCREEN MODE
To switch from the Navigation Bar mode to Full Screen, or vice-versa, just tap once on the bottom part of the screen.
WHO ARE THE FRUIT PEOPLE?
The Info button will reveal more detail about any fruit.
WALLPAPER FUNCTION
You can set any fruit photo as a background Wallpaper. Visit the Photos or Setup app to install this on your screentop.
